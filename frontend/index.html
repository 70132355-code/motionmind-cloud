Frequently Asked Questions

Click the "Start Camera" button on the Dashboard or any module screen. Allow camera access when prompted. Once the camera is active, gesture detection will begin automatically. Follow the on-screen gesture guides for each module.

MotionMind works best on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, and Edge. Ensure your browser has camera permissions enabled and supports WebRTC technology.

Check that your browser has permission to access the camera. Ensure no other application is using the camera. Try clicking "Restart Camera" if the feed appears frozen. If issues persist, refresh the page.

Ensure proper lighting in your environment. Keep your hand clearly visible within the camera frame. Make gestures deliberately and hold them for 1-2 seconds. Avoid rapid movements and ensure only one hand is in frame.

Navigate to the Presentation screen. Click "Choose File" and select your PowerPoint (.ppt, .pptx) or PDF file. Click "Upload" and wait for conversion. Once loaded, use gestures to navigate slides.

MotionMind is optimized for desktop browsers with webcam support. Mobile device compatibility may vary depending on camera access and browser capabilities.

Click the "Logout" button in the navigation bar at the top of the screen. This will end your session and return you to the login screen.
